Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 22 February, 2025

The following document outlines the types of data that is collected, what the data is used for, and what controls are available.


What we collect

Any data you provide into Alidade is stored locally in your own files. If you prefer to store your files in the cloud, you may need to consult your cloud provider's privacy policy.

Otherwise, Alidade does not collect any data on its own. You can also verify this by browsing the source code.


What data is used for

Since Alidade doesn't collect any data, nothing is done on our end.


What you can do

Go on a hike, build a really cool bridge in your world, take up farming... The possibilities are endless.
